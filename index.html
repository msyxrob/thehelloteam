Our Strategy

Unlike traditional answering services, we work closely with our homecare clients to establish call categories and routing protocols before service begins. This ensures that emergency calls are immediately directed to on-call coordinators, while non-urgent calls and payroll inquiries are directed to the appropriate department.
The Hello Team uses the latest technology such as artificial intelligence. This allows our agents to assist your callers with real-time information.

Our AI Technologies

By using our AI technology, we are able to separate calls by category and send them to the appropriate department or person. This ensures that maintenance tickets are directed to the maintenance department, rent questions are sent to the billing department, and any other inquiries are handled efficiently.  Another important feature of our service is the ability to separate calls by building.
